Tour In Gianyar

Museum Seni Agung Rai

Location: Jl. Raya Pengosekan Ubud, Ubud, Ubud District, Gianyar Regency
The Agung Rai Art Museum is a fine art museum in Bali. This museum houses a collection of paintings by famous painters. The paintings collected come from abroad and domestically. The painting that became the flagship was the classic Kamasan painting. The painting was made on bark and masterpieces from the 1930s-1940s. This place is a special attraction for tourists.

Museum Blanco Renaissance

Location: Jl. Raya Campuhan, Sayan, Ubud District, Gianyar Regency
Gianyar does hold a lot of history. Various museums are here. One of them is the Blanco Renaissance museum. This tourist spot is very popular and is visited by many tourists. This place is very thick with Balinese culture. You can enjoy paintings by artists in collaboration with a typical Balinese atmosphere.

Pura Tirta Empul

Location: Jl. Tirta, Manukaya, Tampaksiring District, Gianyar Regency.
Tirta Empul Temple is one of the temples that tourists visit. This place is quite interesting, because there you can find springs. This spring is used by the community for bathing and praying for holy tirta. This place is also a tourist stopover route. You will also stop by this place for a while. So, before your bus trip continues, you have to look around the beauty of this temple.
