Tour In Buleleng

Krisna Adventure

Location: Sukasada, Sambangan, Sukasada District, Buleleng Regency
A vacation in Buleleng is incomplete if you haven't gone to Krisna Adventure. This place is a family tourist ride. Here is a challenging playground. In addition, you will be presented with beautiful natural scenery. If you come to this place, you should bring your family. Not only that, here you can take pictures there. A place that is made instagramable is suitable for you when you are on vacation here.

Air Terjun Melanting

Location: Jl. Melanting Waterfall, Gobleg, Buleleng Regency
In addition to the Sekumpul waterfall, you can also visit the Melanting waterfall. This waterfall also has beautiful nuances. This waterfall is also a favorite of tourists who are crowded with visitors. Here you can take a bath and swim. You can enjoy the chirping of birds and lush trees. You will be hooked and want to keep coming here. In addition, you can take fun photos with friends and family.

Air Panas Banyuwedang

Location: Pejarakan, Gerokgak District, Buleleng Regency
Banyuwedang means hot water. Banyuwedang hot spring is a hot spring. This place is close to the beach. If you run out to the beach, you can pamper your body here. The facilities provided are very good and comfortable for you. You can visit it easily, because of its strategic location. In addition, you will feel the benefits of the hot water, because it is given ingredients that relax the body.

Danau Buyan

Location: Pancasari, Sukasada District, Buleleng Regency
In addition to waterfall tourism and other waters, Buleleng has a beautiful lake. The lake referred to here is Lake Buyan. This lake is one of the three twin lakes there. The lake is the largest. This lake has a pond that is directly connected. By the community, the pond is called the aya lake. Around this lake, you can camp and feel the calm atmosphere around this lake.
