Tour In Badung

Garuda Wisnu Kencana

Location: Jl. Raya Uluwatu, Ungasan, South Kuta District, Badung Regency
This tourist attraction has been famous for quite a long time. Almost every tourist who visits Bali must make this place a destination. So it's not surprising that many local and foreign visitors come on vacation to this tour. Visitors can see the Garuda Wisnu statue and the Vishnu God statue which is very beautifully carved by one of the famous artists in Bali in this place. In addition, usually the sunset view will look beautiful when viewed from GWK.

Sangeh

Location: Sangeh, Abiansemal District, Badung Regency
In Badung there is also an animal tourism park. Here visitors can see the daily activities of the existing monkeys. The number is also very large and left free without a drum. The nature around it is also still beautiful and green. Around this place there are also many stalls that sell various Balinese souvenirs.

Pandawa Beach

Location: Kutuh, South Kuta District, Badung Regency
The beach is also famous among tourists. Apart from its natural beauty that is still well preserved, this beach has its own uniqueness. That is what makes it always attract the interest of visitors who come. When visitors enter the road to the beach, on the left side there will be stone carvings from the pandawas. Air Terjun Nungnung

Location: Pelaga, Petang District, Badung Regency
The first tourist attraction is a waterfall. This place is in the highlands of the mountains. So for visitors who like to explore the beauty of nature, this tour is suitable to be an option. The flowing water is also still clean and clear. The atmosphere there is also very calm and peaceful. Coupled with its natural beauty and nature. Make visitors feel at home to spend time with family or friends here.
